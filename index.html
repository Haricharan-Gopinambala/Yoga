Simple Yoga Asanas with Timings

1. Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Duration: 1 minute

Stand tall, feet together, arms by your side. Focus on breathing deeply.

2. Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Duration: 1 minute each side

Balance on one leg, place the other foot on your thigh, hands in prayer position.

3. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Duration: 30 seconds

Lie on your stomach, lift your chest while keeping your elbows close to your body.

4. Balasana (Child’s Pose)

Duration: 2 minutes

Kneel down, sit back on your heels, stretch arms forward, and relax.

5. Shavasana (Corpse Pose)

Duration: 5 minutes

Lie flat on your back, arms relaxed, close your eyes, and focus on your breath.
